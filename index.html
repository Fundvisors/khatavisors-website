Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a special printer to use KhataVisors?

No — KhataVisors works with any standard Bluetooth thermal printer, the same kind already used in most shops. Pair it once in the app and you're ready to print.

Does the customer need WhatsApp installed to receive invoices?

Yes, the invoice is sent to their WhatsApp number. If they don't use WhatsApp, you can still print a paper receipt as usual — WhatsApp delivery is an addition, not a replacement.

What happens to my data if I switch phones?

Your bills and khata history are backed up, so switching to a new device doesn't mean starting over — just log back in.

Can more than one person use it at the same counter?

Yes, the Shop and Multi-outlet plans support more than one device, so staff can bill from separate phones while everything logs to the same khata.

Is there a setup fee?

No hidden setup fees on any plan. Get 7 days free Trial, Starter is just ₹99 to try with no long-term lock-in.
